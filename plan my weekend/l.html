How This Travel Agency Work ?

A travel agency helps people plan and arrange their trips by assessing their needs, preferences, and budget. Travel agencies can be retail agencies that sell services directly to customers, or they can be wholesalers that create tour packages for retail agencies. Research :- Travel agents research travel options for their clients, such as flights, hotels, cruises, and tours. Plan :- Travel agents create itineraries for their clients, including transportation, lodging, and activities. Advise :- Travel agents provide advice on travel plans, including passports, visas, customs, insurance, and trip cancellation policies. Book :- Travel agents book flights, hotels, and other travel arrangements. Modify :- Travel agents modify reservations if there are delays, cancellations, or other issues. Handle issues :- Travel agents handle any travel issues that arise, such as flight or hotel cancellations, delays, conflicts, and refunds.
